About

Hi! I am Aleksandar Cherganski and this is my personal blog about anything I find interest and have the urge to share with other people. Throughout high school I participated in many mathematical competition (such as the International Mathematical Olympiad) and I can proudly say that by heart I am a mathematician. In university I steered towards the more applied subject of Computer Science with a focus on Machine Learning. My academic interests include: Machine Learning, Probability and Statistics, Computer Vision, Robotics, Graph Theory, etc. My hobbies are: Music (guitar&piano), Juggling, Windsurfing, Volleyball and Blogging (as of recently :D). The blog posts might include any of the above, as well as anything else that comes to my mind. Hopefully, readers might find them interesting!
